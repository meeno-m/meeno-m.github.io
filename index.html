03 — About

The person
behind the build.

Computer science roots, a product mindset, and a decade of shipping.

I'm Meena — most people call me Meenjo. I sit right where design meets development: fluent enough to code it, disciplined enough to design it, and analytical enough to know if it worked.

My path runs from a Computer Science degree in Kabul to a master's in Information Systems in Japan, developer roles with UN organizations — UNICEF, UNCTAD and UNDP — across Asia, and now an MBA in Business Analytics and analyst work in the U.S. I've built for government bodies, non-profits, global agencies and small businesses alike — that range is the point.

What I care about is the same everywhere — products that are clear, honest, and actually get used. If you need someone who can design the thing, build the thing, and read the numbers, that's me.

Off the clock? I'm the girl from the land of lapis lazuli, pistachios & Qabli palaw — proudly Afghan. I cook (really, really well), and I'm always catching random photos — food, light, little moments. There are a few just below.
